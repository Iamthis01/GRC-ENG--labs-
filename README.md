🏛️ OVERVIEW

Welcome to the AI Governance & GRC Lab repository. This is a technical hub for applying Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) frameworks to the lifecycle of Artificial Intelligence systems. Our mission is to codify ethical AI standards into actionable technical workflows.  
🧪 LABS & RESEARCH

All research, labs, and governance projects are managed as Agile Issues to maintain a clear audit trail for system integrity.  

    Repository Workflow: We document all projects, including research findings and technical labs, as tickets and issues within this repository.  

    Active Labs: The Responsible AI Risk Assessment is the first module in a series of planned governance labs.  

    How to Contribute: Please visit the Issues tab to explore all available lab modules and ongoing governance research.
